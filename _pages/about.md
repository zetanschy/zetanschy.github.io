Hello! It’s Jans here :wave:

I’m a robotics engineer at [Robot.com](https://www.robot.com/) (formerly Kiwibot), working on robot learning for humanoid systems.

Before that, I was a research affiliate at [Yale University](https://www.yale.edu/), where I worked on foundation models for brain imaging. I hold a B.Sc. in mechatronics engineering from the [Pontifical Catholic University of Peru (PUCP)](https://www.pucp.edu.pe/). While I was a student, I did research on robot learning for tactile sensing at the [Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems](https://is.mpg.de/) in Germany and built AI-based robotics demonstrators at [Bosch AI](https://www.bosch-ai.com/). I also competed in the [European Rover Challenge](https://roverchallenge.eu/) and the [Latin American Space Challenge](https://www.lasc.space/).
