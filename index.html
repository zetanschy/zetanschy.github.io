Research

I'm interested in computer vision, deep learning, robotics, and multimodal AI. My research focuses on developing intelligent systems that can understand and interact with the physical world through multiple sensory modalities. I work on applications ranging from tactile sensing and material recognition to clinical fMRI analysis and autonomous robotics. Some papers are highlighted.

Adding internal audio sensing to internal vision enables human-like in-hand fabric recognition with soft robotic fingertips
I. Andrussow, J. Solano, B. Richardson, G. Martius, K. Kuchenbecker
IEEE Humanoids, 2025
paper / arXiv

We integrate audio sensing with vision in soft robotic fingertips to enable human-like material recognition through multimodal tactile perception.
